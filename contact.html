Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Aspire2025?

The event is open to all college students from any stream, year, or institution. We encourage cross-college participation!

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in all events is completely free of charge. However, you must register online beforehand to secure your spot.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can register for and participate in as many events as you like, as long as the event timings on the schedule page do not clash.
